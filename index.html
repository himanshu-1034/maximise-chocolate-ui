Maximise Chocolates

Instructions

  1. Fill in the number of rows and columns and hit start
  2. Every block will have numbers. The goal is to collect maximum chocolates
  3. Once the board appears, use arrow keys to move the robots downwards
  4. The robots can only move downwards. It could be diagonally or straight
  5. use the ⬇️ key to move downwards in a single line
  6. use ⬅️ , ➡️ keys to move downwards diagonally
  7. The top score will be located at the top-right corner
  8. The game is over when the robots reach the bottom
